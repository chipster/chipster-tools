Seurat v4 -Subset out anatomical regions based on clusters

Description

This tool filters out anatomical regions based on clusters to focus on a subset of data. The subsetted object is then visualised with the SpatialDimPlot.

Parameters

  • Subset of clusters [1,2,3,4,5]

Details

As input, give a Seurat R-object (Robj).

For more details, please check the Seurat tutorials.

Output

  • seurat_obj.Robj: The Seurat R-object to pass to the next Seurat tool, or to import to R. Not viewable in Chipster.
